Our Story

From the heart of the Swahili coast to your plate, with passion and heritage.

A Taste of Home

Mnyazi Swahili Dishes was founded by Chef Mnyazi, who grew up on the Kenyan coast learning the art of Swahili cooking from her grandmother. Inspired by the bustling spice markets and the fresh catch of the day, she dreamed of sharing her family's cherished recipes with the world.

Our commitment is simple: to honor tradition and flavor. We source our spices directly from East Africa and work with local markets to find the freshest ingredients, ensuring every meal we serve is a true taste of coastal home cooking.
